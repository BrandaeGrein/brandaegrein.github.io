VR Music Room

This was a contract done for MyPad3D, a small VR company based in Seattle, Washington. This is a proof of concept project VR Music Room developed for therapy use at a variety of hospitals across the state, including the Seattle Children's Hospital.

For this project, we wanted users to be in a light, colorful environment to help alievate the stress of long-term hospital stays. Movement is designed to be limited so that users who are bedridden can have equal enjoyment as players who have a bit more freedom of movement. The Music Plane is meant as an educational tool for what sound actually is. Once funding is secured, this project will include a networked room with all the instruments where multiple users can experience playing music together. More features will be added that will add in more usability and fun.
